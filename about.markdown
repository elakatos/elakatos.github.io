I am a computational biologist, interested in applying mathematical and bioinformatic techniques to understand biology in health and disease. I currently work as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Evolution and Cancer Group, Barts Cancer Institute, where I design mathematical models and bioinformatic methods to decode how cancers evolve in response to the immune system and treatment.
